# Our mission

We exist to bring aerospace stories to Canada and the world—with a Canadian editorial lens that celebrates space exploration, asks the questions that matter, and refuses to reduce a multiillion-dollar industry to clickbait.

The universe is expanding. So is humanity's place in it. We're watching rockets launch from three continents, seeing Canadian tech on international missions, reading announcements from startups that will reshape how we live in space. But aerospace news is fragmented: buried in press releases, locked behind paywalls, scattered across Reddit and Twitter, or oversimplified for general audiences. **We unify it.** We add clarity. We ask "why does this matter to Canada?" and we publish the answer every morning.

!!! quote "From Here To The Stars"
    
    We believe aerospace shouldn't feel distant. Whether you're an engineer, a journalist, a student, or just someone who looks up and wonders—you deserve to know what's happening. We publish so you can understand.

---

## Who this is for

You might find us if you are:

- **Aerospace enthusiasts** — space followers who want daily updates beyond Reddit and Twitter
- **Journalists** — reporters covering Canada's role in space, NASA missions, or commercial spaceflight
- **Students** — learning about the industry, careers, or engineering
- **Industry professionals** — tracking launches, competitors, and policy
- **Curious Canadians** — people who wonder what Canada's doing in space, and why it matters
- **Anyone** who believes space is worth paying attention to

---

## What we cover

We publish across several content streams, each designed to answer different questions:

**Daily Broadcasts** — the daily roundup of everything. Launches, discoveries, funding announcements, policy shifts, international cooperation, commercial milestones. All of it, curated into one article so you don't have to scan fifty feeds.

**Weekly Spotlights** — deep looks at specific players. NASA's latest announcements (Fridays), SpaceX's week in the news (Mondays). These go deeper than the daily and tell the narrative of where each organization is headed.

**Monthly Deep Dives** — the long-form features. Canada From Orbit (Week 1 Wednesdays) explores Canadian talent, technology, and contributions to global space efforts. Rocket Lab Roundup looks at the small-lift launch market. Blue Origin tracks commercial spaceflight. Commercial Space captures the broader startup ecosystem.
